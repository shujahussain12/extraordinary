location and calender

Acton Academy Katy is proud to be serving you in 2 convenient Locations!

Katy & Richmond/Fulshear
Farmhouse Campus

Located on the border of Fulshear and Richmond, our Farmhouse campuses is easily accessible to both of these growing communities via Westpark Tollway 1093 and FM 359.

With over 2 acres of property to explore, and 2000 sq. feet of indoor space, our farmhouse campus provides a quiet, serene learning environment.

Our Farmhouse Campus will open Fall of 2022.

9259 Farm to Market 359, Richmond, TX 77406

Flagship Campus

Conveniently located just 2 miles from our previous location, our new Flagship campus sits on over 2 acres of land with lush trees and plenty of room to explore nature.

The interior boasts over 2500 square feet of open space for learners to comfortably spend their days.

4110 Katy-Fulshear Rd.

Katy, TX 77494

hours and tuition

Hours

Acton Academy Katy Preschool, Elementary and Middle School Ages 3-14 Monday-Friday, 8:00 am-3:30 pm

Tuition

At Acton Academy Katy, we believe that quality education should be affordable to all families regardless of income. We work hard to keep tuition low, while providing a state-of-the-art education for 21st century learners. This includes things like: research-based educational software, Montessori materials and practices, expertly trained guides, and small class sizes.

If you have 2 or more children attending Acton Academy Katy, tuition discounts are applied.

  • $7,600 annual billed in a lump sum or over 10 months ($760 per month)

  • $500 non-refundable enrollment fee (First Year Only)

enrichment programs

At Acton Academy Katy, our Enrichment Programs are designed to inspire, educate, transform, and just be down right FUN!

The programs take place each day Monday through Thursday from 2:00-3:00 PM and have included art, sports and fitness, dance, music, coding, and other activities!
